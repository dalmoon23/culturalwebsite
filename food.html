Traditional Melanau Mukah Food

Umai

Umai is one of the traditional foods of Melanau Sarawak. Umai is also a traditional food of the people of Kampung Igan. There are various types of umai produced by the residents of Kampung Igan such as Yellow Fish Umai, Jellyfish Umai, Prawn Umai, Bubok Umai (Small Prawns), Shark Umai and various other suitable fish. There are 2 types of Umai, namely Umai Campur and Umai Cicah.

Pipuih

Pipuih is a traditional food of the Melanau people that is produced from fish that has been ground or crushed and the fish bones removed. Moon fish filling is often used to make pipuih. The fish filling is then placed in the "iseing leaf" (palas leaf). It is then tied and roasted until it is cooked and the filling is dry to ensure the durability of the pipuih filling.

Sagu

Sago was the main food of the Melanau community in the past. Sago is a substitute for rice for a meal for them. There are several types of sago such as Mukah sago, Medong sago and Tupek sago. Among the ingredients used to make sago are lemantak (sago flour), coconut milk, refined dry coconut, salt and rice powder.
